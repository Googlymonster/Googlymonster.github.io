About me

My name is Kevin Nguyen, I graduated from Texas Tech University with a degree in Chemical Engineering in 2013. I worked as a field engineer from 2013 to 2017 before changing career paths into an operations engineer in 2017. From 2017 to 2018 I worked as an operations engineer before an opportunity arose to be a technical services engineer. Currently I'm working as a Compliance Data Specialist doing ETL data work.

I have always loved science and engineering since I was young. I try to find solutions to hard problems and would say I'm an outside of the box thinker. During the years I have continue to learn to expand my skillset as an engineer, which lead me to start coding. Being that my father is a software engineer it helped me fast track my knowledge in this skill. I consider myself a self-starter that is motivated to continue learning all aspects of engineering.

Googly Codes

A blogging website similiar to Medium that is free to use.

Technology

Technology
